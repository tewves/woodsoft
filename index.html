The process flow for mill transfer in WoodSoft is as follows:

  • Identify the Party: In this case, a Party will be created in system called “Kilombero Valley Internal Consumption” and this party is selected
  • Order Contents: In this step, the items in the mill transfer tally are entered into the system. The system behaviour is exactly the same as in the case of Sales Order.
  • Order Summary: In this step, the shift number is captured in the system and the system displays the total order volume and quantity
  • Print Transfer Order: If required, the transfer order can be printed

            In any sized timber production industry round timber is milled to produce sized timber of various dimensions. Round timber is transferred from the yard to the mill in batches for production. The round timber pieces in the various batches are tallied and entered into the system. On doing so, the stock is reduced with contents of the timber transferred to the mill. The business process of round timber sales and transfer works in a very similar manner. In both cases, timber is moved from the yard and the stock needs to be updated.The key differences between these two processes are listed here.

  • In case of sales order the party to be transferred is a customer whereas in the case of mill transfer the party is “Internal Consumption”
  • In case of sales order the selling price needs to be entered whereas in the case of mill transfer the selling price is irrelevant
  • In case of mill transfer, we need to specify the shift for which the transfer is done
  • In case of mill transfer the shipping and payment details are not relevant

With Woodsoft, you can create Transfer Orders. Woodsoft keeps track of the remaining pieces in the inventory.

            The objective of this business scenario is to convert round timber to sized timber. Round timber logs, which are transferred from the timber to the sawmill, are sent through saw milling machines and are sized according to the dimensions given to the sawmill foreman. Then, the timber pieces are grouped as per their size and type, and counted. These pieces are then moved from the saw mill to various storage locations. The various timber size types are already captured in the system.

The storage locations are also captured in the system.The sizing module in WoodSoft captures the timber sizing work order is captured in the system. The results of sizing process is captured in the system where the accurate number of various types of sized pieces are captured. The system calculates the round timber input volume and total volume of the sized pieces. This helps is calculating the wastage of timber during the sawing operation. Using the storage location functionality, it is possible to know exactly where the sized timber is stored in the warehouse.

Also, a sizing work order can be assigned to a manufacturing order using the Transaction ID attribute. This helps in clearly identifying how much round timber was allocated in a manufacturing order and the net yield of the sizing process for meeting the order. In total, the process identifies the input costs in meeting a manufacturing order.

            The most common process in a retail timber saw mill is to get the timber sized as per the customer’s order. As soon as the timber is milled, an invoice needs to be given to the customer to collect the payment. This involves calculation of volume of each piece of timber and then calculating the invoice amount using the volume. The typical process in a retail timber saw mill is to provide milling services to its customers. WoodSoft automates the invoice generation process for timber milling services. It provides an easy-to-use interface, where the end user enters the length and girth of various pieces of timber. Wood Soft picks up the milling price defined in the system, automatically calculating the volume and the milling charges. It generates an invoice that can be printed or emailed to the customer.

WoodSoft supports the invoice generation for the following activities in a timber mill:
  • Milling of Round Timber
  • Planing of Sized Timber
  • Milling of Coconut Trees
  • Hourly Milling Services
  • Sale of Firewood
  • Sale of Sawdust

In addition to the milling services, the additional expenses such as labor charges etc. can also be included in the invoice. WoodSoft also calculates the service tax if necessary . WoodSoft keeps track of the various invoices paid and the outstanding payments as well.
